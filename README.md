# Easy English — Support

**Easy English** is a free English↔Uzbek dictionary that works fully offline. Look up words in both directions, read English definitions, and build your own personal vocabulary list.

This page is the official support and help center for the app. If you have a question, found a bug, or want to suggest a feature, you're in the right place.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an internet connection?
No. The dictionary, definitions, and your saved words all work **completely offline**. An internet connection is only needed for the optional Community features (news and messages).

### Do I have to create an account?
No. Sign-in is **optional**. You can use the full dictionary and "My Words" without any account. You only need to sign in if you want to post in the Community.

### How do I save a word?
Open any word and tap the save/bookmark action. Your saved words appear in the **My Words** tab.

### A word is missing or a translation looks wrong. What can I do?
Our dictionary is always being improved. Please email us at **[mobilchi4862@gmail.com](mailto:mobilchi4862@gmail.com)** with the word and the correction, and we'll review it for a future update.

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?
Go to **Settings** and choose your theme. By default the app follows your device's system theme.

### How do I switch the app language?
Open **Settings** and choose your preferred language (English or Uzbek).

### Is the app free?
Yes, Easy English is free to download and use.

### How do I delete my account or data?
If you signed in and would like your account or community messages removed, email us at **[mobilchi4862@gmail.com](mailto:mobilchi4862@gmail.com)** and we'll take care of it.
